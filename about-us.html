About Us


InventHelp in Pittsburgh, PA, has established itself as a vital resource for aspiring inventors since its founding in 1984. With a proven track record of over 10,000 patents secured, the firm has developed a comprehensive system designed to help innovators navigate the challenging terrain of product development and patent acquisition. By fostering creativity and offering tailored support, InventHelp empowers individuals to transform their inventive ideas into successful commercial ventures.

The services provided by InventHelp cater to inventors at various stages of their endeavors, from conceptualization to patent protection. Key offerings include prototype model creation and technical drawing services, which enable inventors to visualize their concepts and effectively communicate with potential stakeholders. Additionally, InventHelp connects clients with experienced patent attorneys who ensure their intellectual property is safeguarded, a crucial step in ensuring their innovations are not susceptible to infringement.

Beyond its core services, InventHelp prides itself on nurturing an informed community of inventors. Through a wealth of resources, including educational materials and success stories, clients are equipped with the knowledge they need to thrive in the competitive landscape of innovation. With a commitment to supporting creativity and fostering connections, InventHelp stands out as an invaluable partner for anyone seeking to take their inventive ideas to the next level.
